About us

Group D

Dani Santamaria & Tyler Ryu

Our team noticed that early in the semester, the novel coronavirus detected first in Wuhan, China was unlike common viruses and even suspected it could have global implications. Therefore, we chose this topic to inform others outside of Asia in order to keep people safe and to make educated decisions. However, the virus arrived before our final project was due, and now we are doing everything we can to catch up with this ever evolving virus.
